About Us

Melgar farm and Nursery is an orchard farm and seedlings nursery business that started in 2015. They are located in the municipality of Gabaldon in the province of Nueva Ecija. Their farm is surrounded by mountains and waterfalls. They also have a large farm with various crops. They also have numerous farmers who take care of the crops thus make sure that the things provided to you are fresh and delivered on time. All of the fruits and seedlings that we sell are in season and of superior quality. You can rest assured that you will be able to purchase our agricultural products in a convenient way.
